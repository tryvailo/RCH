Telephone Enquiry Checklist

Use this checklist when calling care homes to ensure you gather all essential information

Availability & Immediate Needs

  • 1. Do you currently have beds available?
  • 2. What is your typical admission timeline?
  • 3. Do you offer trial stays or respite care?

Medical & Care Services

  • 4. What level of nursing care is available on-site?
  • 5. Do you have experience managing [specific condition, e.g., diabetes]?
  • 6. How do you coordinate with GPs and specialists?

Costs & Financial Details

  • 7. What exactly is included in the weekly fee?
  • 8. Are there additional charges for activities, laundry, or hairdressing?
  • 9. What are your annual fee increase policies?
  • 10. What deposits or registration fees are required?

Daily Life & Family Involvement

  • 11. What are your visiting hours and policies?
  • 12. What activities and social programmes do you offer?
  • 13. How do you communicate with families about care and wellbeing?
  • 14. Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?
  • 15. When can we arrange a viewing?
