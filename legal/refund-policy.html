Refund Policy

Last Updated: 29 September 2025

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're committed to your satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with your care home assessment report, we'll refund your payment in full within 30 days of purchase.

Our Commitment to You

At RightCareHome, we stand behind the quality of our care home assessment services. We understand that choosing a care home is one of the most important decisions you'll make, and we want you to feel confident in using our service.

Who Is Eligible for a Refund?

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your initial payment
  • You have received your assessment report (we need to understand what didn't meet your expectations)
  • You can provide specific feedback about why the service didn't meet your needs

Important Note:

We do not require you to have purchased a care home placement to receive a refund. Our guarantee is about the quality of our assessment service, not whether you ultimately use one of our recommended care homes.

Which Services Are Covered?

Standard Assessment (£99)

  • Full refund available within 30 days
  • Covers the assessment report and all included services

Premium Assessment (£149)

  • Full refund available within 30 days
  • Includes premium analysis and consultation services

Monitoring Services (£9-39/month)

  • First month refundable within 30 days of sign-up
  • Subsequent months: Cancel anytime, no refund for partial months
  • No cancellation fees

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is straightforward:

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. 1

    Contact Us

    Email hello@rightcarehome.co.uk with "Refund Request" in the subject line

  2. 2

    Provide Details

    Include your order number/email and brief feedback about why the service didn't meet your needs

  3. 3

    We'll Respond

    We'll confirm your refund within 2 business days

  4. 4

    Receive Refund

    Funds returned to your original payment method within 5-10 business days

Refund Processing Times

  • Approval: Within 2 business days of your request
  • Card payments: 5-10 business days to appear in your account
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank transfer: 3-5 business days

Please Note:

The timing of refunds appearing in your account depends on your bank or card issuer. If you don't see your refund after the stated timeframe, please contact your bank first, then reach out to us if the issue persists.

What Happens After a Refund?

  • You will retain access to your assessment report for reference purposes
  • We will not share your personal information with any care homes
  • Any ongoing monitoring services will be cancelled
  • We welcome your feedback to improve our services

Exceptions to Our Refund Policy

Refunds may not be provided in the following circumstances:

  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • If you've used our service to successfully place a loved one in a care home and are simply dissatisfied with that home (our service provides recommendations, not guarantees about individual homes)
  • If you violated our Terms of Service
  • If you've disputed the payment through your bank or card issuer before contacting us (this creates administrative complications)

Partial Refunds

In certain cases, we may offer a partial refund. This typically applies when you've received substantial value from our service but had specific issues with certain aspects. Partial refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Chargebacks and Disputes

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback through your bank. Chargebacks:

  • Take longer to resolve than our refund process
  • May result in additional fees for you
  • Prevent us from understanding and addressing your concerns

If you initiate a chargeback, we will provide your bank with evidence of the services provided and this refund policy.

Free Services and Lead Magnets

Our free services (such as free care home shortlists, guides, and checklists) are provided without charge and are not subject to refunds. However, if you're dissatisfied with these free resources, please let us know so we can improve them.

Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will not affect refund requests for purchases made before the policy was updated. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

Your Rights Under UK Law

This refund policy is in addition to your statutory rights under UK consumer law, including the Consumer Rights Act 2015. Nothing in this policy affects those rights. For more information about your statutory rights, visit Citizens Advice.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please don't hesitate to contact us:

Email: hello@rightcarehome.co.uk

Subject Line: "Refund Request" or "Refund Policy Question"

Response Time: Within 2 business days
