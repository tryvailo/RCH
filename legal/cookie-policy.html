Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 29 September 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how RightCareHome ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at rightcarehome.co.uk (the "Website"). This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide information to website owners, and improve the user experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

1. Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Examples of Essential Cookies:

  • cookieConsent - Stores your cookie consent preferences (Duration: 1 year)
  • sessionID - Maintains your session while filling forms (Duration: Session only)
  • questionnaireData - Temporarily saves your questionnaire progress (Duration: Session only)

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (essential for website operation). These cookies cannot be disabled.

2. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our services.

Analytics Services We Use:

  • Google Analytics - Tracks pages visited, time spent on site, bounce rate
    Cookies: _ga, _gid, _gat (Duration: up to 2 years)

Legal basis: Your consent. You can opt out of analytics cookies through our cookie banner.

3. Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help us deliver more relevant advertising and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Marketing Services We Use:

  • Facebook Pixel - Tracks conversions and enables retargeting
    Cookie: _fbp (Duration: 90 days)
  • Google Ads - Tracks ad performance and conversions
    Cookie: _gcl_au (Duration: 90 days)

Legal basis: Your consent. You can opt out of marketing cookies through our cookie banner.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to the third parties' own cookie policies:

  • Google's Cookie Policy
  • Facebook's Cookie Policy
  • Stripe's Privacy Policy

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Through Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our Website, you'll see a cookie banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer.

Through Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, if you block all cookies (including essential cookies), some parts of our Website may not function properly.

Browser Cookie Management:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Tools

You can also opt out of certain cookies using these tools:

  • Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
  • Your Online Choices (UK)

Cookies and Personal Data

Some cookies may collect personal data (such as IP addresses). This data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and UK GDPR regulations.

Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. Our Website does not currently respond to DNT signals, but you can use our cookie banner to manage your preferences.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
