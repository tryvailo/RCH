Common Questions

What happens if you find a problem at the care home?

You'll receive an immediate alert explaining the issue and how serious it is. Our care experts will provide clear guidance on what to do next—whether that's contacting the home, speaking to the CQC, or considering other options. We'll help you every step of the way.

How is my family's information protected?

We're fully GDPR compliant with bank-level encryption. Your loved one's information is never shared with anyone else. We only collect what's necessary to monitor their care home effectively. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel online anytime with one click—no phone calls, no questions asked. If you cancel within 30 days, you'll receive a full refund. No hassle, guaranteed.

What if everything is fine at the care home?

That's wonderful news! You'll still receive regular "peace of mind" reports confirming that everything is stable. Many families tell us these confirmations are just as valuable as the alerts—it's reassuring to know everything is being monitored.

Do you work with all care homes in the UK?

Yes—we monitor all CQC-registered care homes across England. We track official CQC data, complaints, staffing changes, and publicly available information for any care home you choose. If your loved one's home is registered with the CQC, we can monitor it.
