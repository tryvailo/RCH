Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our care home selection service

Is bed availability guaranteed? +

Not in v1. We highlight homes with recent updates and high likelihood of availability. A bed-verification concierge is coming soon.

Do you take commissions from care homes? +

No. We're independent and fee-only for families. This ensures our recommendations are genuinely in your best interest, not influenced by care home payments.

How does the 48-hour delivery work? +

Once you complete our comprehensive assessment, our care specialists analyse your needs and match them against our verified database. Your personalised report is delivered via email within 48 hours, or you receive a full refund.

What areas do you cover? +

Currently, we provide comprehensive coverage for Birmingham with 277 verified care homes. Manchester, London, and other major cities are launching soon. Contact us for early access in coming soon areas.

How is this different from hospital discharge support? +

We work alongside hospital discharge teams to provide additional independent guidance. While hospital teams focus on immediate placement needs, we provide comprehensive analysis of quality, costs, and long-term suitability for your specific requirements.

What's included in the £99 report? +

Your comprehensive report includes:

  • 5 personalised care home recommendations
  • Detailed comparison table with CQC ratings
  • Hidden costs analysis and fee breakdown
  • Professional visit checklist
  • Complete annual cost calculator
  • Explanations for each match
