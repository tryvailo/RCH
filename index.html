Common questions about our care home selection service

Do you take commissions from care homes? +

No. We're independent and fee-only for families. This ensures our recommendations are genuinely in your best interest, not influenced by care home payments.

Professional Assessment (¬£119) provides complete professional analysis for most families:

  • 17-question personalised matching
  • 5 carefully matched homes
  • 40+ evaluation criteria
  • Complete CQC safety analysis
  • Cost calculator (1-5 years)
  • 48-hour delivery
  • Email support

Premium Intelligence (¬£299) adds deep-dive analysis for complex situations:

  • 38-question deep-dive (2.2x deeper personalization)

  • 156 detailed criteria (3.9x more comprehensive)
  • Daily data updates from 6 sources
  • Staff stability monitoring (LinkedIn + job boards)
  • Financial security analysis (Companies House)

  • Priority email support (24h response)

Choose Premium if: Urgent placement (< 2 weeks), complex medical needs, or want maximum confidence before major investment.

Currently, we provide comprehensive coverage for Birmingham with 277 verified care homes. Additional cities are launching throughout 2025.

What's included in the Professional Assessment report? +

Your comprehensive report includes:

  • 5 personalised care home recommendations matched to your specific needs
  • Detailed comparison with CQC ratings, fees, and transparency on hidden costs
  • Professional visit checklist and 7-day action plan
How accurate is the availability data for local care homes? +

We update our availability snapshot every 48 hours using direct data from care home providers and CQC API feeds. While space commitments can change quickly, our regional page reflects the most recent verified status to guide your initial shortlist.

What makes a 'verified match' different from a simple proximity search? +

A verified match considers over 40 factors‚Äîincluding staffing levels, safety ratings, specialist services and financial stability‚Äîrather than just distance. This ensures each care home aligns with both your location and specific needs.

Can I customise the criteria used for my region's shortlist? +

Yes. When you request a Professional or Premium report, you can prioritise factors most important to you (e.g., dementia care expertise, language support, or budget constraints). Our Smart Algorithms adapt to those preferences.

How often does the Premium Intelligence report update its data? +

Premium Intelligence ingests real-time updates from six authoritative sources‚Äîincluding CQC, NHS Directory, company filings and local authority records‚Äîand refreshes critical insights up to once per day during the first week after purchase.

What if I don't find a suitable home in my postcode area? +

We automatically extend your shortlist to neighbouring postcodes (up to 10 miles) and notify you of newly available options. Our Premium report also flags emerging vacancies in real time.

How secure is my personal information when requesting a regional report? +

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We never share your email or phone number with care homes without your explicit consent, in line with GDPR and UK data protection laws.
