Telephone Enquiry Checklist

Use this checklist when calling care homes to gather essential information

Availability & Care Services

  • Do you currently have rooms available?
  • What types of care do you provide? (personal care, nursing, dementia)
  • Can you accommodate [specific medical condition]?
  • What is the staff-to-resident ratio during day and night?

Costs & Fees

  • What is the weekly fee for the level of care needed?
  • What's included in the weekly fee? (meals, activities, laundry)
  • Are there any additional charges? (hairdressing, chiropody, outings)
  • How often do fees increase, and by how much typically?

Visiting & Trial Stays

  • Can we arrange a visit to see the facilities?
  • Do you offer trial stays or respite care?

3 Expensive Mistakes to Avoid

These mistakes cost Birmingham families £3,000-£10,000 per year. Here's how to avoid them with your free shortlist:

Mistake #1: Missing Hidden Fees

Real cost: £180/week in "extras" not mentioned in advertised price = £9,360/year

✓ How to avoid with free shortlist:

During your phone call, ask: "What's included in the weekly fee?"

Expected answer: "All meals, activities, laundry, and basic care"

⚠ Red flag: "Activities and laundry are extra"

Mistake #2: Choosing Based Only on CQC Rating

Real impact: "Good" rating doesn't mean good fit for YOUR needs

✓ How to avoid with free shortlist:

During your visit, observe: "Do staff interact kindly with residents?"

Good sign: Staff know residents by name, make eye contact, chat naturally

⚠ Red flag: Staff rushing through tasks, no conversation, residents ignored

Mistake #3: Not Checking Specialist Care Capability

Real cost: Moving homes after 3 months = £500 admin fees + emotional trauma

✓ How to avoid with free shortlist:

During phone call, ask: "How do you manage [specific condition - diabetes/dementia]?"

Good answer: Specific protocols, trained staff, equipment on-site

⚠ Red flag: "We can arrange that if needed" = they don't do it regularly
