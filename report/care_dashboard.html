Frequently Asked Questions

How often is information updated?

We check data every week and send you an update every Monday morning. For critical events, you'll receive an immediate notification.

Where does the data come from?

We analyse official CQC reports, family reviews, public records of incidents and complaints, and news about the care home.

What does "Everything's fine" mean?

It means we haven't found any warning signs: no new complaints, incidents, or negative changes in the quality of care.
